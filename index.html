Revealing Sacred Beauty Through Song

Katapetasma - derived from the Greek term for the curtain (veil) separating the altar from the rest of the sanctuary, symbolizing our mission to reveal sacred beauty through the ancient traditions of Orthodox liturgical chant.

Discover Our Mission

A Men's Orthodox Church Music Ensemble

Specializing in polyphonic, male-voice sacred chant, we draw from the oldest Orthodox chant traditions, including Byzantine, Old Russian (Ruthenian), and Georgian monastic melodies in both monodic and harmonic styles.
